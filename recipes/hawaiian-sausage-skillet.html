Hawaiian Sausage Skillet

Description

Easy to make recipe for Hawaiian Sausage Skillet. Was submitted by user elttlbit78. They decided to make this when they couldn't find a recipe anywhere with these ingredients. They suggest serving immediately over rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or more to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package Polish sausage, or to taste, cut into 1-inch squares
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 large green bell pepper, cuit into 1-inch squares
  • 3/4 fresh pineapple - peeled, cored, and cut into chunks
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1/2 cup pineapple juice, or more to taste
  • 1/3 cup packed brown sugar, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
